Can Om and Cows solve for REAL issues?

Photo: ANI

The kind of address Prime Minister Narendra Modi made today in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh was a clear indication that he is back in the election mode which are going to be held in Haryana, Maharashtra and Jharkhand this year and Bihar next year. To put it mildly, if this is what we are going to witness in the forthcoming election rallies by the Prime Minister then this indeed is shocking. His views on Om and Cow were a real shocker today and I condemn what he said.

On one hand, we all talk about “New India”, “Digital India” and “Chandrayaan” and on the other hand the PM is taking us to medieval India. Well, I have personally not come across any religious community who gets a “CURRENT” as mentioned by the Prime Minister on utterances of “OM”. Our country has very vast and different religious flavours which are prominent during the festivals that portray the brotherhood of man. Or is it a narrative being created with the Assembly elections around the corner. The cow of course, is very pious and revered by all Hindus but should this be reiterated every time? The BJP Government should be working silently on the uplifting of this new Cow Ministry and should not be creating an issue about what they intend to do.

There are a few questions to be asked. Why such a media fanfare on this? Why has the PM not spoken yet on the deteriorating Indian economy. On the backdrop of a huge mandate, is this what we should expect of the Modi led government. No jobs, industries being shut down and the latest casualty being Ashok Leyland. Cows and Oms can be taken care of but the indulgence is required in getting back the depressed economy on the track. Only then can we become a 5 trillion dollar economy in the next five years as envisaged by the PM Modi. But as mentioned, we will hear a lot more about these non issues. This is just the beginning. The government is going to up the ante during the forthcoming election rallies which is going to be abysmal. I hope and pray that better sense prevails and there is some solution to the continuous downward trend of economy.
