Clean India Green India

One step taken up by the Government of India for a cleaner and greener environment needs mention. The ban of single use plastics will help enormously for a clean environment. It really is a disgusting site in the morning when you leave for the office and find polythene on the pavements of the road. The foul odour adds more to miseries. Single use plastic largely refers to plastic wrappers, bags, cups, straws which contribute to environmental imbalance. Such plastics can not be destroyed and will only aggravate the problem till it is available.

India produces 25,940 tonnes of plastic waste every day. Of this, 10,376 tonnes of waste is uncollected. One sixth of the total plastic waste is generated by 60 major cities with Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore and Kolkata contributing to more than 50%. In New Delhi, plastic ban is being implemented very strictly. One can observe this as most of the people have started carrying their own cloth bags for groceries, as none or very little plastic bags are available. The Mother Dairy booths have stopped giving polythene bags and now the customers have to carry the milk packets in their hands or other alternatives. But there should be no complaints about it if we want a sustainable environment.

I feel that few measures must be taken before implementing the ban. Alternatives have to be provided to manufacturing units. Waste management has to be improved with regular monitoring and enforcement. Paper bags and discarded cloth material bags can be a useful substitute. Many states like Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Himachal Pradesh and Telangana have started implementing the ban strictly and hopefully other states will follow soon. Prime Minister Narendra Modi, in his address to the nation on 15th August from the ramparts of the Red Fort had urged the nation for a ban of single use plastic. The ban would completely be implemented from 02nd October on Gandhi Jayanti. The Mahatma himself advocated clean environment and healthy living conditions. Let this be a tribute to the Father of the Nation this day.
