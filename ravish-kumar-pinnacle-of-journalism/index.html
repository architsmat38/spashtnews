Ravish Kumar touches the pinnacle of journalism - Conferred the Ramon Magsaysay Award

What a proud moment for Ravish Kumar. We are extremely delighted that the prestigious Magsaysay Award for free and Spasht journalism has been conferred on him. Ravish Kumar is the chief editor of NDTV known for his crisp journalism which has often not gone down very well with the ruling parties. His show, NDTV Primetime is famous and watched by millions on his reports of real life and under reported problems of the downtrodden. He comes from a very humble background from Motihari, Bihar. He owns a post graduate degree in Hindi journalism from Indian Institute of Mass Communication. He was also included in the top 100 most influential Indians 2016 by Indian Express.

He was conferred this award on Monday at a function held in Manila, Philippines. He has often raised voice for the voiceless and is known for his highest standards of journalism. Ravish Kumar is amongst the five recipients of this prestigious award which is Asian synonymous of a Nobel Prize. In his acceptance speech he said that a strong democracy can only grow with fearless journalism and where the truth prevails. His deliberations on various political, socio-economic issues are very incisive. His anchoring of the talk shows is remarkable. His reports are closely watched by all the political parties because they are very close to the true identity of a common man, the very edifice on which the countryâ€™s democracy stands.

Journalism should never be biased and for this his efforts must be applauded for structural and honest reporting. This award will definitely encourage the present and future strings of the young aspiring journalists to take this as a profession. Ramon Magsaysay award recognises the transformative leadership in Asia. Ravish Kumar definitely stands tall amongst the rest of the journalists in India. I wish him the best for his future endeavours.
