Pakistan's Nuclear Threat

After it became clear to Pakistan’s Prime Minister Imran Khan that the USA was not going to intervene in Kashmir, he has become a restless person. It is getting difficult for him to swallow the snub by the USA. In a haste, the Pakistan Prime Minister had announced on Monday that Islamabad would test a ballistic missile today. Islamabad had informed India as per the accord between the two countries in 2005 that mentions a three day prior notice on any missile test.

Pakistan has successfully tested Ghaznavi, their surface to surface missile. Because of this, three air routes of Karachi airspace have been shut down till 31st August, 2019. But this act of Pakistan is not going to perturb India and the Center. New Delhi knows that Pakistan wants to regain lost ground on Kashmir and can go to any extent to grab the attention of the United Nations globally. Maybe the mind bent of China is behind all this because it has already ascertained Pakistan of its support. Pakistan has time and again threatened India of a nuclear intervention, if the Kashmir issue is not solved the way they want. But all this plea to solve this problem is yielding no results internationally even with the support of Beijing.

Ghaznavi is a short range missile and they have two more Ghauri and Shaheen as medium range missiles. Pakistan government has all of a sudden started rushing things. One should see the rhetorics of the Pakistan media persons on their news channels. The desperation is very clear. Pakistan does not even stand half a chance of competing with India’s nuclear warfare. We are far ahead of them and are well equipped but as a peace loving nation we have always restrained ourselves from any such acts which may prove devastating on the either side of the border. If we wish to capitalize on the nuclear warfare, there is none to stop us. India government which has been elected on the back of a huge mandate, gives it the huge authority to curb any sort of cross border terrorism. It will be better for Pakistan to realize sooner or later that it has been completely isolated on the Kashmir issue internationally. This is not cricket, Imran Khan that you go bang bang in the slog overs. I hope that better sense prevails and he should stop crying and begging about Kashmir on the international stage.
