Chidambaram's Tryst With Destiny

What a drama has been created by the top echelons of our society and by that I mean CBI - the most trusted investigating agency of a democratic India. Is the former Home Minister, P Chidambaram, a runaway goon or a terrorist? Well the way CBI was in a raid mood it defied all logic beyond my comprehension. Probably what was served in the tray for the former Prime Minister has left a very bad taste in the present political system.

Why was CBI in such a hurry to arrest Chidambaram or is it a sinister act carried on the instructions of the current government in power? Chidambaram had his own views about the RSS when he was serving his term. Peter Mukherjea and his wife Indrani Mukherjea, owners of INX Media have complained against Chidambaram who have themselves are accused of murdering their own daughter. Have they succumbed to the pressure of the police to malign Chidambaram which requires a definite probe.

Chidambaram has cooperated in all the police/CBI inquiries that have been held against him. He is a politician with a certain fan following. Probably he deserved a better treatment from the CBI personnel rather than jumping on the walls of his house. In my opinion, he has every right to procure legal advice. He himself is a learned statesman and a respected Parliamentarian. Let the law take its own course of judgement. This sounds straight from a Bollywood movie - “You hurt my back, I will hurt yours”. Dirty politics, to say the least.
